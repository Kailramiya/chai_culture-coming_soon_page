## Design Choices (Royal Indian Branding)

The palette uses deep browns, warm gold, and soft cream to evoke heritage, warmth, and premium quality. Serif headings add a classic, regal tone while the sans-serif body keeps the experience modern and readable. Subtle gradients, rounded elements, and restrained hover effects keep the page elegant without feeling heavy.
